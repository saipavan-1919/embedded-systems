TASK:
	Process performed by the system is known as a Task.
	Both process and threads are considered as Tasks only.
	Thread is sub-process of a main processs, so they are known as tasks.
     

PROCESS:
        Process is the execution of a program.
	It is also known as instance of a program.
	Process creates code,data,stack,heap segments in the memory.
	Processes executes paralelly.
	
	

THREAD:
	Small unit of a process is known as a Thrread.
	Thread is a sub-process in a process.
	It only creates stack segment.
	Thread are similar to the functions.
	Threads consist of thread definition,declaration.it does not contain calling.
	Threads runs one after the other
