Critical section:
	
	Critical section is the main section in the thread synchronization using semaphores mechanism.
	We can access the shared resources through critical section.
	There are one section before and after the critical sections they are known as entry section and
	exit section.
	
Deadlock:

	Deadlock occurs When two or more threads are wiating to get the locks which are blocked by other threads.
	if a program has two threads if two threads are waiting then the program doesn't run. so that kind of 
	situation is known as Deadlock.

Race around condition:

	when same information accessed by multiple threads there is a chance of getting unexpected output ,that kind
	of situation is known as Race around condition
